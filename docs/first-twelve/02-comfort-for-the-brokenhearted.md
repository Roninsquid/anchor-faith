# Journey II — Comfort for the Brokenhearted

For grief, loss, heartbreak, loneliness, and mourning.

---

## Opening

If your heart feels heavy today...

Welcome.

Grief, loss, disappointment, and loneliness can leave us feeling as though the world has become quieter than we were prepared for.

You do not have to hide that here.

God is not distant from the brokenhearted.

He draws near.

---

## 📖 God's Word

> The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit.

**Psalm 34:18**

---

## 💭 Pause & Reflect

What loss, disappointment, or burden feels heaviest today?

You do not need to solve it right now.

Simply acknowledge it before God.

---

## 🙏 Pray

Father,

My heart feels heavy.

Some wounds cannot be explained with easy words.

Thank You for remaining near even when I cannot feel Your presence.

Hold what I cannot carry.

Comfort what I cannot heal.

Teach me to trust that I am not walking through this valley alone.

Amen.

---

## 🌱 One Small Step

Take a few quiet moments today.

Read Psalm 34 slowly.

Notice every verse that reminds you God draws near to those who are hurting.

You do not need to hurry through grief.

## Blessing

Go in peace today.

May the Lord be near to your broken heart.

May His comfort strengthen you.

May His presence remind you that you are never alone.
