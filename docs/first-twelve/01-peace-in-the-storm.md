# Journey I — Peace in the Storm

For anxiety, fear, and worry.

---

## Opening

You are not alone.

Fear and worry can feel heavy, especially when your mind will not slow down. Scripture does not shame you for feeling anxious. It gently invites you to bring those burdens before God.

---

## 📖 God's Word

> Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God.

**Philippians 4:6–7**

---

## 💭 Pause & Reflect

What is one worry you can place into God's hands today?

---

## 🙏 Pray

Father, my thoughts are racing, and my heart feels heavy. Help me remember that I do not carry these burdens alone. Give me peace that is deeper than my understanding, and help me trust You one step at a time. Amen.

---

## 🌱 One Small Step

Take one slow breath.

Read Philippians 4:4–9.

Do not worry about tomorrow yet.

Just be here.
